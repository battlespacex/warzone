
WARZONE - BATTLESPACEX | EXECUTIVE SUMMARY

BattlespaceX is a military‑style global conflict monitoring and situational awareness platform built around a high‑performance 3D globe interface.

The platform aggregates publicly available open‑source signals (OSINT) and visualizes global security activity including missile strikes, drone activity, military aviation movement, naval presence, cyber signals, airspace alerts, and anomaly detections.

The objective is to transform fragmented conflict‑related information into a structured visual operational picture.

Key characteristics of the platform:

• 3D global command‑style visualization
• real‑time or near‑real‑time event monitoring
• OSINT aggregation from multiple sources
• military‑style cinematic operational interface
• scalable modular architecture

BattlespaceX aims to become a next‑generation OSINT visualization environment that can support research, media awareness, analytical observation, and strategic monitoring of global security developments.
