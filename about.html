Hey, I’m Waleed, a Canadian photographer based in downtown Toronto, living for noise, speed, thrill, and fuel.

I picked up a camera in 2009 and never put it down. From day one I’ve chased whatever looks wild through the lens: earth-moving machines, motorsports, superbikes, anything loud, raw, and built to ignite my soul. Motorsports and superbikes pulled me in fast – track days, late nights, and me freezing split-second chaos into clean frames.

Over time I got pulled deep into military and aviation visuals, from fighter jets and bombers to full-on defense events. I don’t just show up and snap “cool shots” – I think like a designer and marketer, making sure every image actually works for you across socials, ads, decks, and campaigns.

Off-camera I’m a UX/UI and Product Designer, Chief Creative Officer, Graphic Designer, and Front-End Developer with over two decades of experience. That design brain drives my work: clean visuals, strong storytelling, and frames that actually do a job.

I’m heavy into post-production, grading my images until they feel like detailed, gritty movie stills. If it’s loud, fast, militarish, or just straight-up badass, I’m in. If you’ve got a project, event, or mission in that world, hit me up.
