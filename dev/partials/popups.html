ABOUT BATTLESPACEX

BattlespaceX is an independent, real-time geopolitical and military conflict monitoring platform. We aggregate, process, and visualize open-source intelligence (OSINT) from publicly available sources to provide situational awareness during active conflict zones.

This platform is operated independently and is not affiliated with any government, military organization, or intelligence agency. All data is derived from open-source public information.
