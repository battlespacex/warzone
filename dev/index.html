Meet the shooter.

Hey, I’m Waleed, a Canadian photographer based in downtown Toronto. I live for noise, speed, thrill, and fuel.

I picked up a camera in 2009 and never put it down. Since day one, I’ve chased anything loud, raw, and built to light a fire. Superbikes pulled me in fast, then military visuals followed, from fighter jets and bombers to full-on battlespace machines.

Off-cam, I’m a full-stack UX/UI and product designer, plus a front-end engineer with over two decades of experience. That designer instinct shapes how I shoot: clarity, impact, and story that lands.

If it’s anything straight-up badass, I’m in. If you’ve got a project, hit me up.
